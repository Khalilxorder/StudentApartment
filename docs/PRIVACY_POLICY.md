# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 7, 2024**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Student Apartments ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Profile Data**: Profile picture, bio, preferences
- **Listings**: Apartment details, photos, descriptions (for property owners)
- **Messages**: Communications with other users
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store card details)

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Location Data**: Approximate location for search functionality (with your consent)
- **Cookies**: See our Cookie Policy below

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Match students with suitable apartments
- Facilitate communication between renters and owners
- Process payments securely
- Send service notifications and updates
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers**: Stripe (payments), Google Maps (location), Supabase (database hosting)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger or acquisition

## Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data (`/api/gdpr/export`)
- **Rectification**: Update incorrect information
- **Erasure**: Delete your account and data (`/api/gdpr/delete`)
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object**: Opt out of marketing communications
- **Withdraw Consent**: At any time, where we rely on consent

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@studentapartments.com

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: 30-day grace period, then permanent deletion
- **Backups**: Retained for up to 30 days for disaster recovery
- **Logs**: Retained for 90 days

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Sentry error monitoring (anonymized data)

## Cookies

We use cookies for:
- **Essential**: Authentication, security
- **Analytics**: Understanding usage patterns (anonymized)
- **Preferences**: Remembering language and settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to servers outside the EU. We ensure adequate safeguards through:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (EU approved)
- GDPR-compliant service providers (Supabase, Vercel)

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.

- Sentry: Error tracking (anonymized)
